Welcome

I'm Connor Kenyon, a dedicated Research Computing Facilitator at UMass Dartmouth. With a passion for empowering researchers, and 4 years of HPC Facilitation experience, I specialize in optimizing computational workflows and facilitating access to cutting-edge resources in high-performance computing.
